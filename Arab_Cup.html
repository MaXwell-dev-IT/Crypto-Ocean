Arabic Cup Championship

For the first time in the Arab world, a fully Arab tournament was organized in the world of Play2Earn games within the game Era7: Game of Truth, a Gamefi project that combines Blockchain, Gamefi and Defi in style of Metaverse based entirely on Web3.0.

The Era7 game belongs to the category of card trading games (TCG) that simulate the battlefield with 3 * 3 playing squares for each player, and each of these squares can be occupied by a card which is a NFT that has a market value in addition to the in game usage like attack, defense and unique ability. Era7 game is the first Gamefi in the world that focuses on quick battles since each match lasts for 3 minutes at maximum.

- The start of the tournament was on September 24, 2022, more than 100 players applied to participate in the Arab Championship, and the best 64 players were selected among them to participate in this tournament. Each battle between two players consisted of 3 rounds at maximum, and all matches broadcasted via a special stream in the Arab community on Telegram.

Era7, the world's original three-minute competitive element, is a TCG type game combining Esportsfi and NFT features. By collecting, forming decks, fighting and acquiring steps in the game, players can not only easily get the fun of TCG games in just three minutes, but also earn competitive rewards. Six months after the official launch of the project, Era7 has been ranked among the top projects of BNB Chain through the game's good long-term competitive attributes and deep strategy and playability. It has attracted the attention of many TCG fans and cultivated players' love for competitive elements and daily gaming habits, and the initial PVP competitive ecology within the game has been formed. In the future, Era7 will launch the operation mode with global exclusive competitive ecology, create the brand positioning of "web3 first innovative light competitive long term tournament", shape the unique value of the project in the field of Web3, and fully promote the ecological construction of Esportsfi.

- The second round of the tournament started with 32 players who won in the first round and contained 16 matches also each match consisted of 3 rounds at maximum.

- The fourth round in Arab Championship started on 10/2/2022 and contained 4 battles each battle was between two players of the top 8. This stage included 7 rounds in each match so that the player who won 4 rounds qualified for the next stage. The attendance in the live broadcast was great and full of enthusiasm.

After the end of the Top 4 round in the tournament, two players qualified to the final (Ramez, a Top 150 player from Lebanon + Captain Black, a Top 30 player from Palestine).
